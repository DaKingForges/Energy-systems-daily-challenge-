
RURAL ELECTRIFICATION SCENARIO - RECOMMENDATIONS
================================================

Community: 75 households + school + health center + market
Daily energy demand: 173 kWh
Peak load: 18.6 kW

COMPARISON SUMMARY:
-------------------
         Option  CAPEX (₦M)  Total Lifecycle Cost (₦M)  LCOE (₦/kWh)  Reliability (%)  CO₂ (tonnes/yr)
 Grid Extension       170.0                      247.3         196.0             65.0             53.7
Solar + Battery        88.5                      139.4         110.0             86.4             18.4
    Solar + PHS        56.5                       71.3          56.0             95.4              6.2

KEY FINDINGS:
-------------
- Most reliable: Solar + PHS (95.4%)
- Lowest LCOE: Solar + PHS (₦56/kWh)
- Lowest CO₂: Solar + PHS (6.2 tonnes/yr)

RECOMMENDATION:
---------------
Based on the analysis, the recommended solution is:

SOLAR + PUMPED HYDRO STORAGE (PHS) MINI-GRID

Why: It provides very low LCOE, zero emissions, and long asset life. If suitable topography exists, PHS avoids battery replacement costs and offers durable storage.
ADDITIONAL INSIGHTS:
--------------------
- **Feasibility:** Solar+battery is easiest to deploy anywhere; PHS requires specific terrain.
- **Scalability:** Both mini-grids can be expanded with additional solar panels.
- **Community acceptance:** Solar+battery is well understood; PHS may need education.
- **Maintenance:** Battery needs replacement every 10 years; PHS has minimal maintenance but civil works monitoring.
