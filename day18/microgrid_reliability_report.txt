RECOMMENDATIONS
----------------------------------------
Based on the simulation results and cost-effectiveness:
1. **Demand Response** is the most cost‑effective first step, achieving ~13% ENS
   reduction at minimal cost. Implement immediately with key customers.
2. **Redundant Generator** provides the best balance of reliability gain and cost,
   especially if the diesel generator is a frequent failure point.
3. **Dual Feeder** offers the highest absolute reliability improvement but at higher
   cost – consider for critical loads like the clinic.
4. **Storage Expansion** alone is not cost‑effective unless diesel unavailability is
   the dominant risk and solar penetration is high.
